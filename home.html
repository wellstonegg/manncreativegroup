Who we are

Mann Creative Group brings 44 years of land development and construction experience. We lead the development, land acquisition, debt/capital creation, design coordination, entitlement resolution, and construction for secular and religious organizations in the San Antonio area.

Our history

Our extensive land acquisition, financing, development and construction experience focus includes diverse and complex projects, with four years focused on acquisition and development of large land tracts in San Antonio, Texas. Prior to this, our focus was directed towards “Government Subsidized” and “Special Needs” multi-family housing, and corporate real estate.

Since 2008, Mann Creative Group has devoted time to growing religious organizations; helping manage their real estate needs. To financially supplement this ministry, we have continued our in the commercial sector to allow faith-based projects to be performed with no general contractor overhead or fees.

Our mission

Mann Creative Group seeks to help both new and growing commercial and religious organizations that face tremendous stress and expenses when they try to manage and execute land acquisition, facility relocation, and expansion.

By engaging a single developer/builder, leaders can stay focused on what is significant, while reducing anticipated project costs by 13% - 22%.
